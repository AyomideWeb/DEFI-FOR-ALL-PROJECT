Recently, we could see a huge growing adoption of cryptocurrencies in our countries because of how broken the traditional finance system. However, we noticed that people who are into cryptocurrencies, only buy and hold them speculating with the price. So, my inspiration is building a platform where people can understand, learn and invest in DeFi in a simplified way, in order to introduce them to this amazing world of decentralized finance.
